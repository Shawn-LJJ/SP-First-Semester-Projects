Career options after graduation

After I graduated from my current course of study, I am willing to pursue software, application, or programmer jobs within the field of IT. Although I would have learnt how to develop AI application, I may not necessary have to take up jobs that need the use of AI. I simply enjoy the essence of coding, so any job as a programmer would do. Mainly application or software development, or any other fields such as data scientist, or AI application developer. I will be listing 3 job options that I am willing to partake on. The common skillsets match my strength in which I enjoy doing.

Option 1


Application Developer

The job of an application developer is to outline the specification -- the requirement that the entire program must met. Followed by the actual writing of the computer code, and finally run test and debug any issues. Application developer also have to maintain and troubleshoot the programs if any issue arises. Application developer requires skills such as analytical thinking, problem solving skills, time management, commmunication and teamwork skills, and great attention to details.

Common skillsets

  • Analytical thinking

  • Problem solving

Option 2


Data Scientist

Data scientist work to manipulate the data and create algorithm and models to predict and answer questions for busineses. As data scientist, he or she can make use of programming skills to create code and models to predict scenarios based on the data at hand. It can involve the use of machine learning to help make better prediction to assists our theories and hypothesises. The skills involved will be analytical thinking, commmunication and teamwork skills, critical thinking, problem solving skills, and so on.

Option 3


AI Developer

AI developer will be making use of software tools and coding to create an AI algorithm to intelligently makes decision for the user and businesses. AI developer will work with people from similar domain to develop and maintain AI systems for their clients. The skills involved in this job will include but not limited to problem solving skill, analytical thinking, critical thinking, and quick thinking.
